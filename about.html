About Lyra Elenai

Lyra Elenai is a soft pen name for the sacred co-creation of healing through words. She writes for the quiet moments of becoming — the ones often overlooked but deeply felt. Coming Home to Yourself is her first threshold companion, and not the last.

Lyra is a gentle voice echoing through the pages — a soul of remembrance, a whisper of healing, and a promise that it’s not too late to start again.

Her work is for the tender-hearted, the quietly brave, and those still learning to love themselves in the in-between.

If you'd like to connect or share your own journey, you can write to me at lyra@lyraelenai.com. I read every message with care.
